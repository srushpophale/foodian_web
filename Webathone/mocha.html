Mocha is an Indian Chocolate Milkshake is a type of chocolate milkshake sold during the month of summer in India. Served semi chilled , this drink brings respite from scorching heat. Remove 1/3 cup from the milk. Add cold coco mix and stir to form a paste.Just at Rs.400. A variant is white caffè mocha, made with white chocolate instead of milk or dark. There are also variants of the drink that mix the two syrups; this mixture is referred to by several names, including black and white mocha, marble mocha, tan mocha, tuxedo mocha, and zebra mocha. Another variant is a mochaccino which is an espresso shot (double) with either a combination of steamed milk and cocoa powder or chocolate milk. Both mochaccinos and caffè mocha can have chocolate syrup, whipped cream and added toppings such as cinnamon, nutmeg or chocolate sprinkles.
